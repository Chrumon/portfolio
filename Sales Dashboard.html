Sales Dashboard

Using a sales dataset provided by Quantum Analytics NG, I was able to create this Excel Sales Dashboard. The dashboard was built to provide proper metrics for business decision-making.

Key Insights:
✪ Monthly Sales: May had the highest quantity of items sold, indicating a significant sales peak during this month.
✪ Regional Contribution: The Texas region contributed the most to the total sales value, highlighting it as a key market for the company.
✪ Top Product: Item 1 was the most purchased product, suggesting its high demand and popularity among customers.
✪ Top Salesperson: Anna Weber generated the most revenue among the salespersons, showcasing her exceptional sales performance and effectiveness.

Recommendations:
✪ Understanding customer behavior in May and in the Texas region could provide insights for boosting sales in other months and regions.
✪ The popularity of Item 1 suggests potential for expanding its marketing or inventory to meet customer demand.
✪ Analyzing Anna Weber's strategies could offer valuable lessons for improving the performance of other salespersons.
