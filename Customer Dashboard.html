Customer Dashboard

This Excel interactive dashboard utilizes a Customer Dataset from Quantum Analytics NG, which was structured, cleaned and organized for analysis.
The objective of this analysis is to uncover key revenue trends and customer demographics, including the highest-grossing countries, peak revenue days and the most profitable age groups and marital statuses, to guide strategic business decisions.

Key Insights:
1. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) such as:
a. Sum of Revenue: £21,764,629
b. Maximum Revenue: £5,398
c. Minimum Revenue: £3
d. Number of Country: 10

2. Revenue analysis by country indicates that Australia generated the highest revenue, while Central generated the least.

3. Revenue trends by weekday highlight Sunday as the day with the highest revenue and Tuesday with the lowest.

4. Proportion of Revenue by age group shows that the Adults generated more revenue while the youth generated the least revenue.

5. Revenue by Marital Status represented by a 3-D stacked column chart shows that the married class generated the highest revenue.
The dashboard's interactivity is enhanced through the use of slicers, allowing users to dynamically explore the dataset and visualize changes across various metrics.

Recommendations:
I recommend targeted marketing strategies based on customer segments like age and customer value to maximize returns on marketing investments.
Leverage insights from revenue trends by weekday to optimize promotional activities. As Sunday emerges as the day with the highest revenue, consider launching promotions or special offers during this day to capitalize on heightened consumer spending patterns.
Additionally, consider implementing strategies to boost revenue on lower-performing days like Tuesday.
