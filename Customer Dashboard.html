Customer Dashboard

This is an Excel interactive dashboard using a Customer Dataset provided by Quantum Analytics NG.

The dataset was structured, cleaned, and organized for better analysis. The following are the insights generated;
1. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) such as:
a. Sum of Revenue: £21,764,629
b. Maximum Revenue: £5,398
c. Minimum Revenue: £3
d. Number of Country: 10

2. Revenue by Country represented by a clustered column chart shows that Australia generated the highest revenue while Central generated the least revenue.

3. Revenue trend by Weekday represented by a line chart with markers shows that the highest revenue was generated on Sunday while the lowest revenue was generated on Tuesday.

4. Proportion of Revenue by age Band represented by a pie chart shows that the Adults generated more revenue while the youth generated the least revenue.

5. Proportion of Revenue by Customer value also represented by a pie chart shows that:
Brand Seekers: 17.26%
Casual Buyers: 13.20%
Convinced Seeker: 69.53%

6. Revenue by gender in each Country represented by a clustered column chart shows that Australia has the highest revenue for both genders while Central has the least revenue for both males and females.

7. Proportion of revenue by yearly income range shows that those within the income range of 500001-75000 contributed the highest percentage to the total revenue.

8. Revenue by Marital Status represented by a 3-D stacked column chart shows that the married class generated the highest revenue.
The country field was used as my slicer and upon clicking any of them you will notice the changes on the entire visuals.
