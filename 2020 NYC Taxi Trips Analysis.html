2020 NYC Taxi Trips Analysis

I analyzed a 2020 New York City taxi trips dataset, focusing on green taxis that provide street-hail and prearranged taxi services.

Here is a summary of the insights derived from the analysis:
✪ In 2020, NYC green taxis recorded 1,000,000+ trips, generating $17.89+ million in revenue and facilitating the movement of 1.15+ million passengers across 5 service zones.
✪ The average trip duration was 16 minutes.
✪ The average fare amount for green taxi trips was $12.48.
✪ A total of $206,000 was paid as toll fees.
✪ Credit cards emerged as the preferred payment method for these taxi trips.
✪ January had the highest record for the number of trips, with Fridays being the busiest days, and evenings being the peak time of the day.
✪ The Boro Zone was identified as the most popular pick-up zone.

These insights provide a comprehensive overview of the key metrics and patterns within the 2020 green taxi trips in New York City, shedding light on aspects such as revenue, passenger movement, payment preferences and temporal and spatial trends.
