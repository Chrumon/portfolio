NCDC COVID-19 Report

This report is based on data from the Nigerian Centre for Disease Control (NCDC) website for Week 32 (1st - 4th August 2022).

Key insights as at the time of this analysis:
✪ Covid-19 cases have been reported in all 36 states and the FCT, with Lagos being the main hotspot.
✪ Nationally, there are 262,611 confirmed cases and 3,147 total deaths.
✪ Lagos, as the epicenter, contributes to 69% of total recoveries, less than 2% of active cases, and 57% of total deaths.
✪ Kaduna state has led in testing over the last 14 days, followed by the FCT.
